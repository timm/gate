Module cols.num

Summarize a stream of numbers while maintaining mu,sd $\mu$ mean, $\sigma$ standard deviation).

When (a \ne 0), there are two solutions to (ax^2 + bx + c = 0) and they are $$x = {-b \pm \sqrt{b^2-4ac} \over 2a}.$$
